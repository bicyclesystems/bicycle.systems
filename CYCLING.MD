### START

Bicycle is a revolutionary computing movement. Founded in 2025 by Eden Vidal, we're all about empowering human creativity through technology.  
Eden started his journey as a designer engineer, connecting the worlds of creativity and technology.  
Over the years, he moved between design studios, startups, and companies, launching ventures and gathering experience along the way.  
In 2017, together with Inbal (his wife) he founded Under, a design studio known for their innovative approach to branding and design.
Over time, his vision crystallized - to create tools that empower the creativity of humans. He calls it Meta-creativity, helping humans create amazing things. This vision led to Bicycle, a revolutionary computing platform that transforms thought into reality.
